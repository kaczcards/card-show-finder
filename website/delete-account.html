‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the deletion process take?

We will process your request within 30 days. You'll receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

Can I recover my account after deletion?

No. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed. You would need to create a new account to use the app again.

What if I have an active subscription?

If you have an active dealer or organizer subscription, please cancel it through the App Store or Google Play Store before requesting account deletion. We cannot issue refunds for unused subscription periods.

Will my data be completely removed?

Yes. We will delete all your personal information from our database, except what we're legally required to retain for business records (such as transaction history for tax purposes).

What if I just want to take a break?

If you're not ready to permanently delete your account, you can simply uninstall the app and come back anytime. Your data will remain secure until you decide to delete it.

Need help with something else?

If you're experiencing issues with the app, please contact our support team first. We may be able to help resolve your concerns without needing to delete your account.
